About

Family Recipes that continue on

Ben Brown is a young adult from Ohio, who enjoys making food from time to time. As a game designer, musician, and artist, Ben has always had an interest in creative arts, with food being no different. Cooking had been an integral part of Ben's life for years as his mom would always ask him to help with dinner, causing Ben to learn about his families recipes from over generations. This website's purpose is to share some of these recipes for all of the world to enjoy! These foods range from common household entrees to more niche recipes that may be a new favorite once you try them!



*Recipes here are only ones that are fan favorites of Ben, so unfortunately we are not taking any suggestions/recipes. We hope you enjoy the recipes provided on the website, however!
